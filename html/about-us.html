YOU ARE WELCOME

St.Peter's Anglican church is one of the Churches in the Anglican Diocese of Lagos. We are a community of believers in the Anglican tradition,bound not by our perfection but b our shared needs for God's grace. Our mission is simple; to know Christ and to make Him known. We strive to be a place where your faith can find home, whether you are certain,seacrching or somewhere in between. We extend a particular heart to those who find prayer a struggle. Within our walls and through our ministries,we offer gentle guidance to help every soul find its voice to God.Our foundation is built on the timeless truths of the Holy Scriptures, the powerful sacrements, and the rich liturgical tradiion of the Anglican faith. Here, you will discover a place where ancient worship meets everday life, creating a meaningful and authentic spiritual home. We invite you to come as you are,whether you are firm in your faith, curious,or somewhere in between. You will be welcomed with open arms.
